The cell where X plays on the miniboard determines where player O can play on the metaboard.

For example, if X plays in the bottom left cell of a miniboard...

X

...O must play in the bottom left miniboard on the metaboard.

The same is true after O plays.

One special case: If you're forced to play in a miniboard that's already full, you can play in any empty square on the metaboard.
